The Kuantan Campus was established with the commencement of the medical programme in 1997 and is located at the Jalan Hospital in the town of Kuantan. The campus or rather the Kulliyyah of Medicine was official opened by HRH Sultan of Pahang on 28 November 1997 and is housed in a building next to the Tengku Ampuan Afzan Hospital (HTAA), which is also the main teaching hospital for the kulliyyah. The university decided to establish an administration centre in 2005 for IIUM Kuantan. The administration centre was managed by a Campus Director and a Director (Administration) and was later known as the Office of the Campus Director. There are 18 Departments under the OCD.
